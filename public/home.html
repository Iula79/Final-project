RESIDENZA CARDUCCI

The best accomodation in the city of love

When you enter the walls of Verona you understand why Shakespeare fell in love with this languid and beautiful city.   The Adige river protects the heart of the city with a soft embrace and green hills crowned by ancient fortifications depart from the sides.  At night, the whole city feels like a stage for a dramatic passionate play.   Bed and Breakfast Residenza Carducci provides a setting where you can breathe the history of the city and experience the hospitality of a family that has been here for generations.
